Assignment: Create an artwork where your creative voice is present, something that you are going to be proud to have in your portfolio. Project should build from both aesthetic and technical research this semester to come up with a compelling creative idea that you can develop at your technical level.

Assignment: Take a look at Russian Constructivist art or other artists who work with geometric abstractions like Ellsworth Kelly or Josef Albers, then create a p5 sketch in the style of the painting. However, for your sketch, move all or some of the elements, this could be the a whole geometric form like a rectangle or one of its points.
